# Privacy Policy for Truva VPN

Truva VPN is committed to protecting your privacy. Our service is designed to be a "no-logs" service.

- **No Activity Logs:** We do not collect or store logs of your activity, including no logging of browsing history, traffic destination, data content, or DNS queries.
- **No Connection Logs:** We do not log IP addresses, connection timestamps, or session durations.
- **Data Security:** All traffic is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (VLESS/Reality).
- **Third-Party Services:** Subscription management is handled by the Microsoft Store; please refer to their privacy policy for payment data handling.
- **Contact:** For any questions, please reach out via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/omercnsz/TruvaDesktop
